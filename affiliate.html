Your Postback URL

Add your tracker's postback URL below. When a conversion is recorded for you, the network will fire a GET request to your URL with the macros replaced with real values.

Set different postback URLs per offer. Leave empty to use your global postback.

Available Macros

Use these macros in your postback URL. We'll replace them with real values when a conversion fires.

MacroDescriptionExample
{click_id}Unique click identifiercmn9j020wgd4g7xbn
{payout}Your payout amount45.00
{offer_id}Offer ID17
{txn_id}Transaction / Order IDBG-892341
{sub1} - {sub5}Your custom sub IDs from tracking linkfacebook, campaign1

https://yourtracker.com/postback?clickid={click_id}&amount={payout}&offer={offer_id}&txn={txn_id}
